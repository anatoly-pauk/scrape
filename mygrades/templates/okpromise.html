What is Oklahoma's Promise?

Oklahoma's Promise offers qualified Oklahoma students FREE COLLEGE!

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Must be an Oklahoma resident.
  • Must apply in the 8th, 9th or 10th grade.
  • The parent's federal adjusted gross income must not exceed $55K per year.

    • Even if you do not meet the income guidelines today, you may later. EVERYONE NEEDS TO APPLY.

    Go to the Oklahoma's Promise Website and click the Red button in the top-right hand corner to apply! Make sure you write down your username and password!
