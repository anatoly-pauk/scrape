Driver's Education

There is a "parent taught" online Driver's Education. It is not free. The learning fund will pay for a driver's education curriculum for high school credit.

In the event that there are no funds available, here is another, zero-credit option: Online Driver's Education

Before you can obtain a driver's permit, for either option, you will need to complete this parent affidavit.

E-mail the completed affidavit to parent@dps.ok.gov and they will stamp it. You must take this stamped affidavit with you to take the written test.
